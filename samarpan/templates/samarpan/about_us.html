About Blood Bank

We take proud to inform you that the Samarpan Blood Bank started by North East Zone Chemists Educational & Welfare Trust floated by a trust of chemists situated in between Mankhurd-Chembur, Kurla to Mulund area by Shri.Dilip A. Mehta & Shri.Damji M. Palan as a founder trustee/s in the year 1998 from scratch with sheer "Determination" & "Faith" to today's level by fulfilling the faith shown by the chemists who had contributed 100% fund required for starting up of blood bank project.

A unique concept of service to humanity in professional way
- By the Chemists - For the People

Samarpan in continuation with mission statement ensures easily accessible and adequate supply of safe and quality blood, blood components collected and procured from a voluntary non-remunerated regular blood donor in well equipped premises, which is free from transfusion transmitted infections, and is stored and transported under optimum conditions and issues it at following Not for Profit charges and always ready to help deserving person by making policy that no one should suffer for blood due to want of even reasonable charges and thus making blood available first then comes rest.

♦

Started in 1998 from Ghatkopar-East, upgraded with component & aphaeresis facility and shifted to Sarvodaya Hospital, Ghatkopar-West on 15th Aug' 2007 with new collaborative name as Sarvodaya Hospital Samarpan Blood Bank.
♦

3500' carpet area premise-state of art blood bank.

Completed 22 years/ 4200+ camps/ 495500 + units/ 2200 + Aphaeresis
♦

After compliance of mandatory criteria & tests for blood donation like HIV / Hepatitis-B / Hepatitis-C / MP / VDRL, Samarpan does,

◊

Hemoglobin estimation / B.P. Check up / Blood Grouping / Complete Blood Count (CBC) / Blood Sugar test for 35+ above age / Thalassaemia, in case of suspect due to low HB & other parameters.

◊

Donors are re-tested and confirmatory tests are done for Hepatitis B / Hepatitis C and referred to National Liver Foundation for further counseling by renowned Gastro Enterologist under Federation of Bombay Blood Banks project.

♦

Provides reservation services in case of planned surgery for rational use of blood and to stop wastage of precious blood.

♦

Motivates donors to become single donor platelet-aphaeresis donor even once in a year during June-Oct due to heavy need of platelets.

Thalassemia Day Care Center

♦

Samarpan has started Thalassaemia Day Care Centre on 7th October 2008 by taken responsibility of 10 children's in 1st phase, to facilitate them blood, blood transfusion at blood bank, all required pathological tests, distribution of required medicines without any charges. Since 2008 till the date ATR is around 0.5% throughout the year. We have Increased 159 Thalassemia children .Those who already were infected with HCV we conducted HCV treatment project twice.
♦

Considering need of ADR (Adverse Drug Reaction) monitoring concept, started issuing feedback form for Doctors for Transfusion Reaction database, if any to be returned to blood bank within 48 hours for follow up and creating ADR database.

♦

Top priority is given to the blood requirement for the patients suffering from Thelassaemia & Dialysis.
