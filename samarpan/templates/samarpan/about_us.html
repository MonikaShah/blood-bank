Why Samarpan?

A trendsetter in the entire West Zone of India with all facilities under one roof:

♦

♦

Thus, SAMARPAN ensures easily accessible and adequate supply of the safest and superior quality blood & blood components collected and procured from voluntary non-remunerated regular blood donors in well-equipped premises, which is almost free from infections and is stored and transported under optimum conditions at 'Not-for-Profit' charges. Samarpan is always ready to help any deserving person as per its policy.

Our motto is "No patient should suffer for want of blood".

About Blood Bank

We take pride in informing you that the Samarpan Blood Bank was started by the North East Zone Chemists Educational & Welfare Trust in 1998, with sheer "Determination" & "Faith" to fulfill the faith shown by the chemists who had contributed 100% fund required for starting the blood bank project.

A unique concept of service to humanity in a professional way - By the Chemists - For the People.

Samarpan continues to ensure easily accessible and adequate supply of safe and quality blood, collected from voluntary, non-remunerated donors, stored and transported under optimum conditions, issued at 'Not for Profit' charges.
