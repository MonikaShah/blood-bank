How to Organize a Blood Donation Camp

Contact Blood Bank:

1. Our PRO team will take the follow up and call you for the requirements to arrange a blood donation drive.

2. When you decide to arrange a blood donation drive you need to do some preparations like-

  • Suitable date for the camp
  • Adequate place with proper ventilation and aeration.
  • If it is a bed camp the place should be enough for min 4 blood donation chairs.
  • If it is a mobile van camp then proper place for parking of mobile van which should not disturb other cars to and fro and parking. If possible electric supply for the mobile van.
  • For both the camps arrangement of washroom for the blood donation drive team should be done.
  • Promotion and motivation for blood donation is necessary prior 2-3 days of camp.

3. Your preparations for camp and cooperation from Blood Bank PRO team makes the camp successful.

Detailing of the Camp:

Organizers are request to give every detail like:

  • Confirmed Date of camp.
  • Capacity of hall or venue of camp.
  • Number of donors expected.


Promotion and motivation for the blood donation.

Organizing team is advised to advertise the Blood Donation camp with proper details like Date, Time and Venue to attract the attention of prospected blood donors.









ON THE DAY OF BLOOD DONATION

  • Being an organizer you or your one of the coordinator should be present at camp site before the blood donation drive team arrives.
  • Make sure for the required arrangements and things to be used in camp from your end.
  • Conduction of camp should be under the observation and presence of organizer.
  • Your suggestions for conduction of camps are most welcome for our improvement.
