Welcome to Samarpan Blood centre

We take proud to inform you that the Samarpan Blood Bank started by North East Zone Chemists Educational & Welfare Trust floated by a trust of chemists situated in between Mankhurd-Chembur, Kurla to Mulund area by Shri.Dilip A. Mehta & Shri.Damji M. Palan as a founder trustee/s in the year 1998 from scratch with sheer "Determination" & "Faith" to today's level by fulfilling the faith shown by the chemists who had contributed 100% fund required for starting up of blood bank project.


A unique concept of service to humanity in professional way
- By the Chemists - For the People


Samarpan in continuation with mission statement ensures easily accessible and adequate supply of safe and quality blood, blood components collected and procured from a voluntary non-remunerated regular blood donor in well equipped premises, which is free from transfusion transmitted infections, and is stored and transported under optimum conditions and issues it at following Not for Profit charges and always ready to help deserving person by making policy that no one should suffer for blood due to want of even reasonable charges and thus making blood available first then comes rest.
